Walk the user through setting up their family office. Ask questions one at a time:

1. Ask their name and where they are based
2. Ask about their main assets (property, investments, crypto, cash) and rough total
3. Ask about their top 2-3 financial goals
4. Ask about monthly living expenses
5. Ask about risk tolerance

After gathering answers, create profile.md with their information and one file per asset in entities/ directory.
