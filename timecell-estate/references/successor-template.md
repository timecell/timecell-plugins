## Crypto Literacy
- level: [none / basic / intermediate / advanced]
  - none: Has never used cryptocurrency. Doesn't know what a wallet or private key is.
  - basic: Understands Bitcoin conceptually. Can use a simple wallet app. Knows "don't share your seed phrase."
  - intermediate: Has used hardware wallets. Understands multisig concept. Can follow technical instructions.
  - advanced: Self-custodies their own crypto. Understands key management, security, and recovery procedures independently.
